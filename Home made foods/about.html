_About Us_

Welcome To Home Made Food

We believe that whole food is the cornerstone of health, and that the happiest moment of the day happen at the dinner table. We think ingredient sourcing is important for the planet,for our guts, and because you need fewer things and simpler recipes when the components stands on their own. While many of our recipes are relatively virtuous and free of comman allergens, they are still delicious and simple.
